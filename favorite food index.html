Fried Rice

Fried rice is a classic chinese dish that mostly appears on restaurant menus. The delicious jumble of rice and mix-ins also boast a long, illustrious history which cannot be ignored. Fried rice was originally invented in china and today, it is one of the most delicious meals in its own rights. Over the years, fried rice has evolved to anybody all over the world. There are various renditions of freid rice that draw from cultures and cuisines of countries such as Thailand, Japan and even the US have also originated it.

  • Boil water to steam the rice
  • Put the rice inside the steamed water
  • Wash the rice and put in a sieve
  • put pot on fire
  • add 5ml of oil
  • add onions
  • add all seasonings
  • pour water on the seasoning
  • Mix the rice with the seasoning
  • allow 10 minutes for the rice to mix and get soft
  • turn and check if thr rice is soft
  • bring the rice down from the gas
  • fried rice is ready
